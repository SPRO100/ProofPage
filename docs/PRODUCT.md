# ProofPage product definition

## Promise
One public page that proves what a founder builds and, when connected, what projects earn.

## Language
First-time visitors see a centered English | Русский choice. The selection is saved and remains switchable.

## Free
- One profile and one project
- Base theme and ProofPage branding
- Manual revenue labelled unverified

## Pro
- Multiple projects and themes
- Read-only revenue connections
- Verified badge, source, sync time, and charts
- Advanced analytics

## Trust
Manual revenue never receives a verified badge. Verification displays provider, period, and last sync. Failed or stale connections change public status. ProofPage is not connected to ProofMRR in the MVP.
